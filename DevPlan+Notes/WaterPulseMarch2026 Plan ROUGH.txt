Historical Data
-Saves a daily average of each level and flow rate at each location.
-Today's Averages (each updated reading is recorded throughout the day (level and flow-rate, from 00:00 - 23:59 at each location), average is taken, recorded in database, then "Today's Average" is deleted and starts recording the new day's readings.)

APIs

Finding the Water Flow and Levels:
Alberta
Used fully custom APIs, scraping the rivers.alberta.ca website.

Home-Made Station Info Scrape (to be parsed twice a day.  Database is updated.  Previous data is not saved.  Gives station locations + flood alerts and other important stuff)
https://rivers.alberta.ca/DataService/ListStationsAndAlerts

Home-Made Flow and Level Scrape (parsed once every hour)
https://rivers.alberta.ca/apps/Basins/data/figures/river/abrivers/stationdata/${station.stationType}_${station.dataType}_${station.stationNumber}_table.json
